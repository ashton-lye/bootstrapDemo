Setting Up Slack

Set Up Your Slack Account

Within the IT Team and some of your courses we use slack to communicate.
To create your account simply go to https://to-bcs.slack.com and sign in with your Google Apps Account.

Slack on Your Mobile Device

Slack has a (mobile) app for every platform basically.

Simply go to https://slack.com/downloads and it will detect what kind of system you are using.
If you are on an iOS or Android device, you look up slack in the App Store or Google Play Store.
